{% trans "PayPal Settings" %}

To enable PayPal you must authorize PayPal to allow Collector City LLC to perform the activities below.
  1. Click on the Setup Paypal button at the bottom of these directions. You will be redirected to a paypal login page.
  2. You must sign in with your paypal account or create one.
  3. PayPal Will ask you to Complete your Service Setup.
  4. You will allow Collector City LLC permission to:


    • Retrieve your name.
    • Retrieve your email address
    • Issue refunds from your PayPal account
    • Start Express Checkout payments.
    • Retrieve Express Checkout prepayment details.
    • Complete Express Checkout payments.
    • Authorize card payments on behalf of your customers.
    • Capture transactions that your buyers authorized.
    • Extend authorization of card payments on behalf of your customers.


  5. You must hit "Agree and Continue"
  6. You should be redirected back to the Admin Console.
